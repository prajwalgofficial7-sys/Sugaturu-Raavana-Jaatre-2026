📅 MONDAY 09 March 2026 · 09:00 PM

ರಾವಣ ವಾಹನೋತ್ಸವವು ಈ ಕ್ಷೇತ್ರದ ಅತ್ಯಂತ ವಿಶಿಷ್ಟ ಮತ್ತು ವೈಭವಯುತ ಉತ್ಸವಗಳಲ್ಲಿ ಒಂದಾಗಿದೆ. ರಾವಣನು ಶಿವ ಭಕ್ತ ಮತ್ತು ಮಹಾ ತಪಸ್ವಿ ಆಗಿದ್ದರಿಂದ, ರಾವಣ ವಾಹನ ಎಂದರೆ ಸ್ವತಃ ರಾವಣನೇ ಶಿವನನ್ನು ಹೊತ್ತು ನಡೆಯುತ್ತಾನೆ ಎಂಬ ಭಾವ.ಶಿವ ದೇವಸ್ಥಾನಗಳಲ್ಲಿ ರಾವಣನ ವಾಹನದ ಮೇಲೆ ದೇವರ ಉತ್ಸವ ಮೂರ್ತಿಯನ್ನು ಮೆರವಣಿಗೆ ನಡೆಸುವ ಸಂಪ್ರದಾಯ ವಿಶೇಷ ಮಹತ್ವ ಪಡೆಯುತ್ತದೆ, ಇದು ಭಕ್ತಿ, ಶರಣಾಗತಿ ಮತ್ತು ಶಿವನ ಮಹಿಮೆಯನ್ನು ಸಂಕೇತಿಸುತ್ತದೆ.

13-Day Festival Schedule

Day 01
🏳️
25 FEB 2026 · WEDNESDAY · NAVAMI
ಅಂಕುರಾರ್ಪಣ · ಧ್ವಜಾರೋಹಣ
Ankurarpana · Flag Hoisting (Dhwajaroha)

Sacred seeds sown (Ankura) + festival flag hoisted on the temple chariot street. Special pujas, abhishekas. The Suraturu Valli Nayaka Janangada participate.

🪷 Sri Rama's Message
"Every great journey begins with a righteous intention. As Sri Rama set forth from Ayodhya with dharma, we begin with devotion." — Bala Kanda
Day 02
🦁
26 FEB 2026 · THURSDAY · DASHAMI
ಸಿಂಹವಾಹನೋತ್ಸವ
Simhavahanotsava — Lion Vehicle Festival

Lord Ramaswami rides the Lion (Simha) vahana — symbol of divine power and royal courage. Balé Seetharamayya, Acchappa, Narayanappa & community join the procession.

🪷 Sri Rama's Message
"Courage is choosing dharma over comfort. As Rama faced Ravana fearlessly, true strength lies in righteousness." — Yuddha Kanda
Day 03
🐒
27 FEB 2026 · FRIDAY · EKADASHI
ಹನುಮಂತವಾಹನೋತ್ಸವ
Hanumantavahanotsava — Hanuman Vehicle Festival

Lord Rama carried on divine Hanuman vahana — most beloved procession. Panchanana, Vishwanathacharyaru, Sri Harsha, Hariprasad & Sudamacharyaru join in service.

🪷 Sri Rama's Message
"Hanuman's pure devotion is the ideal path. Those who serve with love and surrender achieve the highest." — Sundara Kanda
Day 04
🐍
28 FEB 2026 · SATURDAY · DWADASHI
ಶೇಷವಾಹನೋತ್ಸವ
Sheshavahanotsava — Cosmic Serpent Vehicle Festival

Lord Rama on Adishesha — the divine cosmic serpent representing eternity and cosmic sustenance. Large Aadiveshya community with 10+ families participate in service.

🪷 Sri Rama's Message
"As Shesha eternally supports the cosmos, a righteous leader eternally supports his people. Service to others is service to the Divine." — Ayodhya Kanda
Day 05
💍
01 MAR 2026 · SUNDAY · TRAYODASHI
ಕಲ್ಯಾಣೋತ್ಸವ + ರಾತ್ರಿ ಗರುಡ ವಾಹನೋತ್ಸವ
Kalyanosava (Wedding) + Night Garuda Vahanotsava

Divine wedding of Lord Rama & Sita reenacted with joy. Evening: Garuda (divine eagle) Vahanotsava under stars. Jyotinanagara community leads the Kalyanosava.

🪷 Sri Rama's Message
"Sita is My very soul, My dharma, and My truth. To honor one's beloved is to honor the sacred bond of dharma." — Bala Kanda, Sita Swayamvara
Day 06
🐘
02 MAR 2026 · MONDAY · CHATURDASHI
ಗಜವಾಹನೋತ್ಸವ
Gajavahansotsava — Royal Elephant Vehicle Festival

Lord Rama on the royal Elephant vahana — symbol of wisdom and auspiciousness. Karadavara N. Krishnapparaju, Venkataramanraju, Janardhanraju & Vinodharaju join in service.

🪷 Sri Rama's Message
"True wisdom is knowing when to act and when to wait. Patience combined with right action — this is the path of the wise ruler." — Ayodhya Kanda
Day 07
🎡
⭐ GRAND CHARIOT DAY
03 MAR 2026 · TUESDAY · 11:00 AM
ಬ್ರಹ್ಮರಥೋತ್ಸವ — ಕರ್ನಾಟಕ ಸರ್ಕಾರ
BRAHMARATHOTSAVA · Gangadreshwara Utsava · Night Chitra Gopu Rath

Karnataka Govt sponsored Grand Chariot at 11 AM. Morning Gangadreshwara Utsava. Night Chitra Gopu Rathotsava. Gonasaami International Artists · Avinash Melodies Orchestra · 14 Generators.

🪷 Sri Rama's Message — Chariot of Dharma
"The true chariot of victory is made of truth, courage, and righteousness. Ride that chariot and no enemy can defeat you." — Yuddha Kanda
Day 08
🐎
04 MAR 2026 · WEDNESDAY · PADYAMI
ಅಶ್ವವಾಹನೋತ್ಸವ · ಶಯನೋತ್ಸವ · ಪಾವರ್ತೋತ್ಸವ
Ashwavahanotsava (Horse) + Shayanosava + Pavartotsava

Lord Rama on splendid Horse — Kshatriya valor. Shayanosava (sacred resting) and Swing festival also conducted. Lekt D. Munegouda family and Sri Ramegouda family lead service.

🪷 Sri Rama's Message
"A warrior's strength lies not in weapons but in moral resolve. Rama chose exile over injustice — honor is mightier than any sword." — Ayodhya Kanda
Day 09
🦢
05 MAR 2026 · THURSDAY · BIDIGE
ಹಂಸವಾಹನೋತ್ಸವ · ವಸಂತೋತ್ಸವ ಪಾನಕ ಪೂಜೆ
Hamsavahanotsava (Swan) + Vasantotsava + Panaka Puje

Lord Rama on Swan (Hamsa) — pure knowledge and discernment. Vasantotsava Spring festival with sacred Panaka drink offering. Gouda N.S. Nagarajgouda leads the service team.

🪷 Sri Rama's Message
"Discernment is the swan that separates truth from falsehood. Always choose the path of truth." — Kishkindha Kanda
Day 10
🎪

Prushtha Pallaki Utsava — Reverse-Facing Palanquin Procession

Unique procession: Lord Rama in palanquin facing backwards — the Lord's protective gaze upon devotees following behind. Gu. Gudegouda, Krishnappa, Motegouda & large family groups participate.

🪷 Sri Rama's Message
"I never abandon those who take refuge in Me. Even as I walk ahead, My gaze of protection always remains upon My devotees." — Yuddha Kanda

ಡೋಲೋತ್ಸವ
Dolotsava — Sacred Swing Festival

Lord Rama & Sita placed on a beautifully decorated swing to devotional songs and music. Bheemanna Nanjundappa, Shriramappa, Someshekhargouda, Erapappa & Nagabhushana team serve.

🪷 Sri Rama's Message
"In joy and sorrow, in life's swinging fortunes — remain steady in devotion. The swing of dharma always rests in God's grace." — Aranya Kanda

👑
08 MAR 2026 · SUNDAY · PANCHAMI

Sri Rama Pattabhisheka Utsava — Royal Coronation Ceremony

Sacred coronation of Lord Rama reenacted in full glory. Sugaturu Aadijambhava Janangada devotees actively participate. Royal Powerflute Friends & Avinash Melodies Orchestra perform. Night Prasada by Munikidirappa family.

🪷 Sri Rama's Message — Ramarajya
"In Ramarajya, no subject suffers hunger or injustice. A king exists only to serve his people — their happiness is his dharma." — Uttara Kanda

Sri Rama's Journey & Life Teachings
ಶ್ರೀ ರಾಮನ ಜೀವನ ಸಂದೇಶ — ರಾಮಾಯಣ
Bala Kanda · Book 1
Divine Birth & Breaking the Bow

Born to Dasharatha in Ayodhya, Sri Rama learned sacred arts, protected sages, and broke the Shiva Dhanush at Mithila to win Sita's hand.

🪷 "Learn from elders, respect teachers, and approach every challenge with a calm and focused mind." — Guru Vishwamitra to Rama
Ayodhya Kanda · Book 2
Noble Exile — Dharma Above Throne

Rama voluntarily accepted 14 years of exile to honor his father's word — choosing dharma over the greatest kingdom. His sacrifice became humanity's greatest lesson.

🪷 "My father's honor is more precious to me than any kingdom." — Sri Rama to Lakshmana
Sundara Kanda · Book 5
Hanuman's Devotion — Power of Bhakti

Hanuman leaped the ocean to Lanka with pure devotion, found Sita, and brought hope. Rama's tearful gratitude revealed how God values sincere love above all.

🪷 "Nothing is impossible for one who carries the name of Rama with true faith." — Sundara Kanda
Yuddha Kanda · Book 6
Battle of Lanka — Triumph of Truth

Rama built a bridge across the ocean and led the Vanara army to Lanka. Ravana's fall — despite great power — proved that adharma always collapses before truth.

🪷 "Every arrow I release carries the weight of truth." — Sri Rama before the final battle
Uttara Kanda · Book 7
Ramarajya — The Golden Age

After coronation, Rama established Ramarajya — every citizen lived in joy and prosperity. He placed public welfare above personal happiness — the eternal standard of leadership.

🪷 "Build Ramarajya in your own soul first — through truth and compassion." — Uttara Kanda
Sri Kodanda Rama · Sugaturu
The Divine Archer of Kolar

Sri Kodanda Ramaswami — the presiding deity of Sugaturu, seated on the banks of Palaar River — is worshipped as the embodiment of righteousness, protection, and infinite grace.

🪷 "ರಾಮೋ ವಿಗ್ರಹವಾನ್ ಧರ್ಮಃ — Rama is the embodiment of Dharma" — Valmiki Ramayana
